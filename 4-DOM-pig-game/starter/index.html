Trevor's Dandy Dice Game

The Rules:

  • The game has 2 players, playing in rounds
  • In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as he whishes. Each result is added to his points
  • BUT, if the player rolls a 1, all their points are lost and their turn is over.
  • The player can choose to 'BANK', which means that their points for the round are added to the scoreboard and his/her turn is over.
  • The first player to bank 100 points wins the game
